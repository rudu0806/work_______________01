About This Product

Ashwagandha, known as "Indian Ginseng," is one of the most powerful herbs in Ayurvedic healing. Our premium capsules are sourced from organic farms in India and processed using traditional methods.

This adaptogenic herb has been used for over 3,000 years to help manage stress, boost energy, and improve concentration. Each capsule contains pure Ashwagandha root extract standardized to high withanolide levels.

  • Certified organic Ashwagandha root extract
  • Standardized to 5% withanolides
  • No artificial colors or preservatives
  • Third-party lab tested for purity
  • Vegan-friendly vegetable capsules
  • GMP certified manufacturing

How to Use

Recommended Dosage: Take 1-2 capsules daily with water, preferably with meals.

Best Time: Morning and evening with food for optimal absorption. For sleep support, take 1 capsule 30-60 minutes before bedtime.

Duration: For best results, use consistently for at least 4-8 weeks. Ashwagandha works cumulatively in the body.
